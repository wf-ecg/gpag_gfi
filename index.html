As we approach the last part of the year, I am delighted to see how GPAG has become such an important “fabric” to our business, that continues to be woven into the daily challenges in this industry. The quality of our speakers, both external and internal to Wells Fargo, and most importantly the participation of our customers, provides us with the most current and relevant information that is critical to our responsibilities.

As we discussed in June, we are about to launch both the GPAG Dialogue Newsletter, as well as our first LinkedIn social media application. Combined with our upcoming Sibos dinner, webinars, and in-person meeting in June, we are truly reaching our goal of working together as a global community, by staying abreast of the continuous changing environment, enabling collaboration, knowledge and connectivity between member participants.

I am confident that we have created the premier group of payments professionals in the world.

As always, I look forward to our interchange throughout the upcoming weeks.

Ashish Sharma
