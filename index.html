On behalf of Wells Fargo and Global Payment Services, we send you warm wishes for 2013 and hope that the year brings you much success. While the year has changed, the challenges facing our industry remain and continue to grow. As we enter the world of Dodd Frank 1073 and increasing compliance and regulatory concerns, we are fortunate to host the Global Payments Advisory Group where we have the opportunity to learn, share and grow among the leading experts in our industry.

Please find the agenda for our upcoming quarterly conference call on January 18 below. Our annual meeting is scheduled for June 9–14 in San Francisco and you will be hearing more about this in the coming months. In the meantime, we urge you to send us suggestions for topics and speakers for any of our meetings.

We are off and running, and we look forward to another successful year working together.
