The third annual meeting of the Global Payments Advisory Group was held in San Francisco, CA from June 10th to the 13th and was considered by all to be a great success. This year’s conference was attended from all four continents by some 36 representatives from a dozen countries spanning Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Americas. Thanks to all of you who attended and made this event such a success.

According to Ashish Sharma, "We are fortunate to have a group of customers so knowledgeable and dedicated to this industry. We value their expert and diverse input". Per George Doolittle, "We have created a community for international bankers to share ideas and carry the contact and friendships beyond the meetings and conference calls. It was evident from this meeting and previous ones, that we are very much inter‑dependent and what affects one area of the block often has unintended or unforeseen affects around the world. GPAG has created tangible value, for both our customers as well as Wells Fargo."
