Delivering on promises is always a good thing. When we met in June, we discussed enhancing the value of GPAG by developing some new initiatives that would further enhance our collaboration. I am delighted to announce that our inaugural edition of the GPAG Dialogue has been published. You should have already received it, but we are posting it to the resource library on this site as well. As I wrote in the welcome section, what makes this newsletter different from most is that it is written as a collaborative effort between all of us: the GPAG community, Wells Fargo leaders, and industry subject matter experts. There are so many issues and concerns in todayâ€™s payments market, that we attempted to come up with a format that allows us to focus on multiple subjects, in a concise and readable format. We gladly welcome your suggestions and input for future issues.

Additionally, we have launched our LinkedIn site, and have already begun to see a flurry of thoughtful and extremely timely posts, again, from both Wells Fargo and our customers. As was clearly reinforced at Sibos, our market only continues to become more complicated, all the more reason for us to join together, via multiple communication methods, to stay in touch and share our daily challenges.

We will be hosting one more webinar before the end of the year, and then, will start building the agenda for our June meeting. In the meantime, let me wish you the best as we approach the holiday season.

Ashish Sharma
