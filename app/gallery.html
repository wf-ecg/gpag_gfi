Annual meeting in San Francisco (June 2014)

There is no question that our June meeting met every objective and plan we had from GPAG since its inception. The quality of our topics, presenters, off-site activities, and most importantly, all of you, continues to position GPAG as a priority for Wells Fargo. I am attaching some photos from the meeting, in the hopes we will remember a few days very well spent. The Wells Fargo team looks forward to seeing you at SIBOS and to planning our meetings for the year ahead. — Ashish Sharma
