Global Payments Advisory Group Asia Regional Banking Conference

  

< Back to GPAG home

Wells Fargo’s Global Financial Institutions group is hosting the inaugural Global Payments Advisory Group Asia Regional Banking Conference in Hong Kong. At this exclusive event, you will meet with fellow senior business leaders, hear from experts on best practices and trends affecting financial institutions, gain valuable insights into emerging technologies impacting global payments, and have the opportunity to network with industry peers and leaders.

Date:
Thursday, March 17, 2016 – Friday, March 18, 2016

Location:
Island Shangri-La Hotel
Pacific Place, Supreme Court Road
Central, Hong Kong

If you are interested in attending this event, please send an email to globalpaymentsadvisorygroup@wellsfargo.com or contact Ashish Sharma.

Included is the tentative agenda to the conference.
